Geometry-Grounded Video Generation: Bridging 3D Structure, Temporal Coherence, and Controllability

Alina Verroth

Preprint · December 2025

PDF

Abstract

Diffusion-based video generation models have achieved impressive visual realism, yet long-horizon temporal coherence and geometric consistency remain challenging. This paper argues that explicit 3D geometry provides a principled structural scaffold for stabilizing video generation over time. The authors review geometry-grounded video generation approaches, connect them to world models and controllable synthesis, and position explicit 3D representations as a unifying abstraction for video generation, editing, and immersive applications.
